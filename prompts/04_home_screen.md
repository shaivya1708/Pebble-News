# Step 4: Home Screen

## Prompt

```
Create home screen with two tabs (like Twitter):

1. Tab 1 - "Top Headlines": Shows breaking news
2. Tab 2 - "For You": Shows news based on user's selected categories

Each news article shows:
- Thumbnail image
- Title
- Short description

Tapping an article opens its details.
```

## What You Get

- ✅ Two-tab home screen
- ✅ News cards with images
- ✅ Personalized "For You" feed
