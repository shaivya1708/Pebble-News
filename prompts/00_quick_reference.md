## üìã Presentation Script

### 1. Login & Register
**Show:** Register a new account, then login
**Say:** "Users create an account with email and password. The app remembers them for next time."

### 2. Home Screen
**Show:** Scroll through Top Headlines, switch to For You tab
**Say:** "Home has two feeds - Top Headlines for breaking news, and For You for personalized content."

### 3. Article Details
**Show:** Tap article, scroll, tap heart, tap bookmark, tap Read Full Article
**Say:** "Users can read articles, save to favorites, bookmark for later, or open the full article in browser."

### 4. Search
**Show:** Tap search, type "technology", tap a result
**Say:** "Search lets users find specific news topics."

### 5. Settings
**Show:** Open settings, check/uncheck categories, save, show For You updates
**Say:** "Users customize their news preferences. This changes what appears in the For You feed."

### 6. Saved Articles
**Show:** Open Favorites, open Read Later from settings
**Say:** "All saved articles are organized in Favorites and Read Later lists."

### 7. Multi-User
**Show:** Logout, register different user, show empty favorites
**Say:** "Each user has their own separate saved articles and preferences."
